# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 6/25/2025

## Age Requirement (COPPA Compliance)
This service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. If you are under 13, do not use this service.

## What We Collect
We only collect the chat messages you send to provide the chat service. We do not collect IP addresses, personal information, or any other data.

## How We Use Your Messages
Your messages are used solely to provide the chat functionality. We store only the most recent 500 messages - older messages are automatically deleted. **All messages are public and visible to all users.**

## Data Sharing
Since all messages are public, there is no private data to share. Messages are visible to anyone using the service.
